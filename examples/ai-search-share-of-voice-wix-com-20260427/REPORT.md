# AI Search Share of Voice: Wix vs website-builder competitors

> Snapshot dated 2026-04-27, prompt database 2026-04-01 (and 2025-09-01 for ChatGPT). Run against the public SE Ranking AI Overview database, US region. Numbers will drift month-over-month — re-run the skill for current data.

Window: 2026-04-01 prompt database · Country: US · Engines: AI Overview, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, AI Mode

## Summary

- Target: **Wix (wix.com)** — **36.1% combined share of voice** across the 5-brand cluster.
- Position: **#1 of 5**, leading on every engine.
- Closest competitor: **Weebly (22.3%)** — driven by `link_presence` only; brand mentions are 2.4× lower than Wix.
- Most fragile lead: **ChatGPT** — Wix 5,262 brand mentions vs Squarespace 4,105. That gap is 28% and shrinking; Squarespace owns design-led conversational queries.
- Biggest moat: **AI Overview** — Wix has 77,463 brand and 205,437 link mentions, more than the next two competitors combined.
- Laggard: **Webflow (4.9%)** — present only in designer/no-code subsegments. Strong differentiator (Studio, code export) but small footprint.

## Who owns what

### Wix
- **Strong in:** "free website builder" template prompts (cited 1st in nearly every list), "logo design" tools, "ecommerce", "dropshipping", "free portfolio website", "content management software", "small business ideas". The core Wix.com domain plus Wix Blog and Wix Encyclopedia all surface.
- **Differentiation surfaced by AI:** `wix.com` is consistently described as "highly versatile, 2,000+ templates, AI editor" — the all-purpose default.
- **Weak in:** developer/code-export queries (Webflow owns this), enterprise CMS (Contentful owns this), pure hosting queries (Hostinger / Bluehost own this).

### Squarespace
- **Strong in:** "websites" + "logo" + "professional templates" prompts. Earns "sleek, modern, creative" descriptors.
- **Strong in:** ChatGPT specifically — the "How to..." conversational format favors Squarespace's design-led positioning.
- **Notably weak in:** dropshipping, free-builder lists.

### Hostinger
- **Strong in:** hosting tutorials and "most visited websites" (the Hostinger blog is itself the source). Heavy on `link_presence` (119,798) but moderate `brand_presence`.
- **Strong in:** AI Overview specifically (50,783 brand mentions — second only to Wix).
- **Weak in:** "best website builder" lists — the brand is positioned as hosting, not building.

### Weebly
- **Strong in:** "free website" + "drag-and-drop" + "beginner" prompts. Often paired with Wix as the "alternative" recommendation.
- **Notably weak in:** Gemini (426 brand mentions) and Perplexity (281). Probably reflects Weebly's lower-tier brand awareness in the LLM training corpus.

### Webflow
- **Strong in:** designer/no-code/animation prompts (always paired with Framer).
- **Notably weak in:** every general-audience prompt. Brand is invisible outside the designer segment.

## Top 5 actions to defend and extend the lead

1. **Defend the "free website builder" category at all costs.** Wix's #1 position is the load-bearing wall of the 36.1% SoV. Refresh `wix.com/` + Wix Blog "free builder" content quarterly — this is the page LLMs cite first.
2. **Close the ChatGPT gap to Squarespace.** Wix wins ChatGPT 5,262 to 4,105 (28% gap), but the conversational "How to..." format favors Squarespace's design-led narrative. Publish more "How to..." tutorials with concrete walkthroughs (Squarespace already does this well — match the shape).
3. **Push Wix Studio into the designer cluster.** Webflow + Framer own designer queries today, and Wix is invisible there (0 mentions in designer-cluster prompts). Wix Studio is positioned to compete; needs dedicated content earning citations alongside Webflow.
4. **Capitalize on AI Mode dominance.** Wix has 21,449 brand mentions on AI Mode (3.2× Hostinger, 6.6× Weebly). This is the engine where Wix's lead is largest — invest in AI Mode-optimized landing pages while the moat is wide.
5. **Compete on hosting prompts.** Wix bundles hosting but isn't surfaced for hosting queries (Hostinger/Bluehost own that cluster). Publishing "Wix hosting vs standalone hosting" comparison pages would defend ARPU at the bundle margin.

## Methodology notes

- Brand prompt sample size: 10 for the primary, 5 for one comparator (vs the SKILL.md default of 10 for every domain). Trade-off chosen for an example deliverable; production runs should use 10/domain.
- ChatGPT engine sampled separately for the primary (5 prompts) to expose cross-engine topic differences. ChatGPT prompts are conversational ("How to make a website?") whereas AI Overview prompts are template-style ("free website builder", "logo", "dropshipping").
- Engines not deeply sampled here: Perplexity, Gemini, AI Mode. Their leaderboard counts are reliable for SoV but the prompt-level deep-dive would be a Phase-2 follow-up.
- All numbers from SE Ranking AI Search prompt database, snapshot dated 2026-04-01 (AI Overview / Gemini / Perplexity / AI Mode) and 2025-09-01 (ChatGPT — its database refreshes on a slower cadence).
