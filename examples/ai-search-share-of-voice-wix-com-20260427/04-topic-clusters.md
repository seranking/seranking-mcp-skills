## Ownership mechanics

### What Wix owns (and how)
- **Free website builder cluster.** Wix's `wix.com/` homepage and Wix Blog are cited in nearly every "free builder" template prompt. The cited URL is consistent (`wix.com/`) — this is one page doing enormous work. Refresh quarterly.
- **Encyclopedia cluster.** `wix.com/encyclopedia/definition/*` is a content moat. Definitional queries ("what is X") at 47-301k volume each cite the encyclopedia. **No competitor in this cluster has built a comparable asset.**
- **ChatGPT conversational tutorial cluster.** Wix's "How to..." blog content gets cited first by ChatGPT in 4 of 4 sampled prompts.

### What Wix is in but doesn't own
- **Logo design.** Canva and Adobe Express are #1/#2; Wix is a credible #3-#5. Improving here means out-publishing Canva on logo-specific content, which is a much harder fight than the website-builder category.
- **E-commerce.** Shopify is the gravitational center. Wix is the credible "if you don't want Shopify" option. Defending this position is reasonable; trying to displace Shopify is not.
- **SaaS CMS.** Wix and Squarespace share the SaaS-CMS sub-cluster. Both win here together.

### What Wix is invisible in (real gaps)
- **Designer / no-code professional.** Framer and Webflow own the "designer" descriptor in every prompt that reaches this cluster. Wix Studio could enter but needs dedicated content earning citations alongside Webflow.
- **Pure hosting.** Hostinger owns the hosting-tutorial cluster (`hostinger.com/tutorials/*` is a citation engine). Wix is invisible there. Probably acceptable — Wix doesn't compete on standalone hosting — but worth confirming with strategy.

## What this cluster grid implies for content strategy

1. **Defend the encyclopedia.** It's an underrated asset. The 246,000-volume "guarantee" prompt cites `wix.com/encyclopedia/definition/guarantee` — every general-knowledge definitional query is a free citation as long as the encyclopedia exists and stays indexed. **Don't deprecate it.**
2. **Refresh the free-builder homepage quarterly.** This single page is doing more SoV work than any other URL in the dataset.
3. **Build Wix Studio's GEO footprint deliberately.** Today Wix is invisible in designer queries. The product exists; the AI-search content moat doesn't.
4. **The ChatGPT conversational pattern is a genuine moat.** Wix wins 4-of-4 sampled "How to..." prompts. Continue investing in conversational tutorial content.
5. **Don't over-invest in logo or hosting clusters.** The competitive structure favors specialists (Canva for logo, Hostinger for hosting). Wix's all-in-one positioning works against it in single-feature categories.
